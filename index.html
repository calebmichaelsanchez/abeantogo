About Us

We have been roasting beans together for 10 years. From humble beginnings in the basement of our own house to roasting at our location in Goodrich, our love for coffee and the customers we serve has grown exponentially.

Our namesake comes from a story in The Bible (Daniel Chapter 3) where three men (Shadrach, Meshach, and Abed-nego) make a choice to stand against the ruler and make a choice to do what is right. Give it a read while enjoying your favorite ABeanToGo brew.
